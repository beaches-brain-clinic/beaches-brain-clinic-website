Adult Assessment · Across the lifespan

Clarity about how your brain works

We offer psychological and neuropsychological evaluation of mental health and cognitive skills to support individuals with confirmed or suspected non-progressive neurological and neurodevelopmental conditions.

What we offer

Three ways we can help

01

Comprehensive cognitive assessment

We assess a range of different brain skills to determine cognitive strengths and weaknesses, inform rehabilitation and support, and guide multidisciplinary treatment planning.

02

Changes in cognition over time

We evaluate changes in brain skills over time — for example, recovery from brain injury, pre- and post-surgery, and medication trials.

03

Diagnostic evaluation

We offer diagnostic evaluation of mental health and neurodevelopmental concerns, providing answers and a clear path forward.
