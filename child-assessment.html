Child & Adolescent · Ages 6 and over

Helping young minds learn, grow and thrive

We provide developmental and cognitive assessments for attention, learning and social-communication difficulties, as well as concerns regarding emotional and behavioural functioning — for children aged 6 years and over.

What's included

Assessments tailored to your child

Depending on your questions and goals, an assessment may draw on one or more of the following.

01

Intelligence testing

Used to identify cognitive areas of strengths and weaknesses, giving a clear picture of how your child thinks and learns.

02

Psychoeducational assessment

Used to identify areas of academic achievement requiring support, and for the purpose of specific learning disorder identification.

03

Neuropsychological evaluation

Assessment of memory, attention and executive functioning to support diagnosis, rehabilitation, support needs and treatment planning.

04

Psychological evaluation

Evaluation of depression, anxiety and other mental-health concerns to support wellbeing and assist with treatment planning and referral.
