About the founder

Dr Natalie Phillips

Clinical Psychologist & Clinical Neuropsychology Registrar — and the clinician behind Beaches Brain Clinic.

Dr Natalie Phillips is an endorsed Clinical Psychologist with more than fifteen years' experience assessing and treating adults and children. Her clinical work focuses on the assessment and rehabilitation of attention, executive function, memory and learning across the lifespan.

Research & practice

A clinician who keeps science close

Active researcher

Serves on the editorial board of the peer-reviewed journal Neuropsychological Rehabilitation, and has presented at national and international conferences.

International experience

Completed postdoctoral fellowships across Australia, Canada and America, and assessed country children with complex conditions at Royal Far West.

PhD in brain injury

Her doctoral research examined the cognitive rehabilitation of working memory in children with traumatic brain injury.

Practice philosophy

Dr Phillips emphasises evidence-based practice — providing clients with assessments and treatment approaches that are backed by science.
