About the clinic

Led by Dr Natalie Phillips

Dr Natalie Phillips is an endorsed Clinical Psychologist with more than ten years' experience assessing and treating adults and children with a range of emotional, behavioural and cognitive difficulties.

Her clinical work focuses on the assessment and rehabilitation of attention, executive function, memory and learning across the lifespan — spanning neurodevelopmental conditions such as ADHD, ASD, learning disorders and FASD, and neurological conditions including traumatic brain injury, epilepsy, MS and dementia.

  • PhD & Doctor of Clinical Psychology, University of Sydney; Master of Clinical Neuropsychology, UNSW.
  • Serves on the editorial board of the peer-reviewed journal Neuropsychological Rehabilitation.
  • Postdoctoral fellowships across Australia, Canada and the USA; assesses children with complex needs at Royal Far West.

How it works

1

3
